Fun Fact:


The First Computer Game Didn’t Make Any Profits

Spacewar! There were some earlier examples of computer games, such as “OXO” in 1952 and “Tennis for Two” in 1958. However, a lot of historians credit “Spacewar!” to be the first true digital computer game. This game is the brainchild of a team of computer programmers from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Steve Russell, Martin Graetz, and Wayne Wiitanen conceived the idea in 1961.
